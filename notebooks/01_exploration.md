# Initial Data Exploration

This notebook performs an initial exploration of historical urea price data in the Black Sea market.
The goal is to understand structure, patterns, and limitations of the dataset before any modeling.

## Dataset Overview

The dataset contains historical price observations for the Black Sea urea market.
At this stage, the focus is on understanding time coverage, frequency, and data quality rather than predictions.
## Key Questions

- What is the overall price trend over time?
- Are there visible seasonal or cyclical patterns?
- Where does volatility appear to increase?
- Are there missing values or anomalies in the data?

- ## Analytical Approach

This exploration prioritizes interpretability over model performance.
Simple visualizations and descriptive statistics are used to form initial hypotheses about market behavior.
